About Us

Divine’s Touch was founded in Johannesburg, Turffontein in 2018 by an entrepreneur; Divine Mussa, a former mobile technician who noticed a growing gap in the local market for reliable , affordable, and trustworthy repair services. What began as a small one- man cell phone repair workshop operating in a neglected building in Turffontein quickly earned a loyal following for its fast turnaround times, honest diagnosis and genuine spare parts. In 2025, Divine and his team took a bold step of launching a modern hair salon and barbershop under the same roof. The beauty division was designed to complement the tech side. The business soon evolved into a full retail service hub, adding trendy clothing and accessories to cater for the everyday needs of its community.

Vision and Mission:

The vision of Divine’s Touch is to be the leading one stop lifestyle destination in South Africa where technology, fashion, beauty senselessly come together. The main mission or main mission statement of the organisation is to deliver exceptional quality services and products in cellphone and electronic repairs, retail of phones, electronics and clothing as well as professional hair salon and barbershop services.
